## Section 8: faqAccordion

- **eyebrow:** QUESTIONS
- **heading:** Daycare FAQs

### Q: What's the difference between full day and half day?
A full day covers our complete operating hours. A half day is approximately 5 hours. Both give your dog full access to play groups, outdoor yards, and indoor space.

### Q: What vaccines are required for daycare?
All daycare guests must be current on Rabies, DHLPP, and Bordetella. Please bring proof of vaccination or have your vet send records before your first visit.

### Q: What is a trial day?
Every new dog spends their first visit as a trial day. We evaluate temperament, play style, and group fit to ensure a safe experience for everyone. Trial days are charged at the regular daily rate.

### Q: Do multi-day packages expire?
Yes. The 10-day package is valid for 2 months, the 20-day for 4 months, and the 30-day for 6 months from the date of purchase.

### Q: What are your drop-off and pick-up hours?
Monday through Friday: 6:00 AM – 11:00 AM and 1:00 PM – 7:00 PM. Saturday and Sunday: 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM. The facility is closed to drop-offs and pickups from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM on weekdays.
