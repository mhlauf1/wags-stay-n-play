## Section 7: faqAccordion

- **eyebrow:** QUESTIONS
- **heading:** Daycare FAQs

### Q: What's the difference between full day and half day?
A full day covers our complete operating hours. A half day is 5 hours or less.

### Q: What vaccines are required for daycare?
All dogs must be current on Rabies, Distemper, and Bordetella. Must be spayed/neutered after 7 months of age (unless vet states otherwise).

### Q: What is the assessment?
Every new dog spends their first visit as an assessment day ($20). We evaluate temperament, play style, and group fit over a minimum of 4 hours. Assessments are scheduled Monday through Thursday, 10 AM – 2 PM.

### Q: Do you offer cat daycare?
Yes! Cats have their own dedicated room separate from dogs. Cat daycare is $18/day.

### Q: What are your drop-off and pick-up hours?
Monday through Saturday: 6:30 AM – 7:00 PM. Sunday: 8:00 AM – 12:00 PM and 2:00 PM – 6:00 PM.
