## Section 7: faqAccordion

- **eyebrow:** QUESTIONS
- **heading:** Transportation FAQs

### Q: What areas do you serve?
We serve select areas across St. Louis and Franklin County, MO. Service area boundaries depend on scheduling and availability. Contact us with your address and we'll let you know if we can get there.

### Q: Can I use transportation for any service?
Yes. Transportation is available for daycare, boarding, and grooming clients. Just add it when you book your service through the portal.

### Q: How does the multi-trip package work?
Purchase a 5-trip or 10-trip package and each ride is deducted from your balance. A "trip" is one direction — so a round-trip pickup and drop-off uses two trips.

### Q: What if I'm not home for pickup?
We can work with you on arrangements — a garage code, a neighbor, or another solution. Just let us know when you book so we can plan accordingly.
