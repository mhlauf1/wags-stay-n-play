# Boarding

All our boarders are in daycare play groups during the day; due to this, each boarder must pass an assessment before staying with us.

At Wags Stay N' Play, we offer four different kennel sizes, allowing you to pick the accommodation that works best for your pet. Dogs from the same family may board in the same kennel if desired, or they may have their own.

Our boarders wake up at 6:30am when they are taken out to go potty. Once inside, they are given breakfast while we create the play groups for the day. By 9am the first group is up and getting to play. Play groups go from 9am until 5pm when boarders are laid down and given supper. Between 8pm and 10pm, boarders are taken outside before bed to have a chance to go potty and drink water.

All dogs must be up to date on Rabies, Distemper, and Bordetella vaccines along with being spayed or neutered after 7 months of age, unless otherwise stated by your veterinarian.

## Cat Boarding

Cats board in our cat room designed just for them. Each cat is given time to roam the room throughout the day and gets individual attention from staff. Cats from different families are kept separate. Due to being separate from each other, there is no assessment needed to board your cat or to bring them to daycare.

Cats must be up to date on Rabies and Distemper vaccines.
