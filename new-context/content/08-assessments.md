# Assessments

ALL dogs must be up to date on Rabies, Distemper, and Bordetella vaccines along with being spayed or neutered after 7 months of age, unless otherwise stated by your veterinarian.

Assessments are scheduled Mondays thru Thursdays from 10am–2pm. We ask for the dog(s) to be here at least 4 hours, but longer is completely fine and there is no extra fee. Assessments are $20 per dog.

During the assessment we let the dog acclimate to the environment before starting to introduce them to the staff and other dogs. Once they're starting to feel comfortable, we introduce them to dogs at their pace. Some dogs need more time than others, which is why we ask for at least 4 hours.

The assessments allow us to discover which dogs get along well with your dog along with making sure this environment will not be too stressful or overwhelming for your pup.

Assessments are required for all daycare and boarding dogs.
